Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Bhilai was established in the state of Chhattisgarh by Ministry of Human Resource and Development in the year 2016. IIT Bhilai is presently housed in its transit campus at Government Engineering College (GEC) Raipur, Chhattisgarh.
The Central Library of IIT Bhilai started and resides in the institute's Academic Building in the year 2016 with the opening of this institute.
